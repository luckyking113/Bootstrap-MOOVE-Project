The Platforms

Fax-Only Infrastructure

Been struggling to find a reliable fax solution? We feel your pain. That's why Moove was built. You see, most VoIP providers look at fax as just an add on service. A box to be checked on the feature list. Voice and fax don't mix. Our solution is designed and built specifically for fax. It's built for carrier and enterprise level scalability in mind

Automatic Cloud Storage

One of the big benefits of moving to cloud faxing is that every fax sent and received is automatically stored securely in the cloud. Never lose another fax. Tag your faxes to be searched latter. Access your fax history from any device.

Virtual Fax Numbers

Every Moove plan comes with a virtual fax number. Faxes that come to this number are automatically sent to you via email and stroed securely in th cloud. Have an existing fax number you want to keep? No problem. You can have your existing fax number ported over and continue to use it with your Moove service plan.

Security and Compliance

Moove is built with regulatory compliance in mind. We can help you get (or stay) HIPAA compliant. We use advanced encryption and security technology to protect your data. User authentication, encrypted perimeter defense, and routine data backup are just a few of the things we do to keep your data secure. Contact us if you need us to sign a BAA.
